Detailed Description

Correlates events.

The correlator class keeps track of where and when implantation and decay events have occurred, and then correlates each decay with its corresponding implant. A decay will only be validate if it occurred close enough in time to the implant, and the implant was well separated in time with regard to all other implants at the same location
